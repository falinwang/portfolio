About Me

I am a UX/Interaction Designer from Taiwan and am now based in Los Angeles, California. I graduated with a Master of Health Informatics, focusing on consumer health technologies and UX design at the University of Michigan — Go Blue!

Design thinking and traveling equipped me with the empathy, curiosity, and passion to talk to people I would have never crossed paths with. I LOVE to learn about how people live their lives. And UX taught me how to translate my observation of complex problems into designs that make people’s lives better.

As a product UX designer, I enjoy working with a cross-functional team to solve puzzles and make great products by empathizing with people, learning the data, and challenging the limit.
